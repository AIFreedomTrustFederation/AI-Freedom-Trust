The umbrella

A clear home for every active initiative.

AI Freedom Trust Federation is organized as a federation of projects, not a pile of disconnected repositories. Each effort has a role: public trust, cloud infrastructure, applied business systems, digital stewardship, and long-term research. The work stays open, accountable, and useful when each repository has a defined purpose and a visible path to maturity.

Agents, automations, and infrastructure should remain accountable to people, consent, and clear operating rules.
